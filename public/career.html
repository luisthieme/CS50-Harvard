SCHOOLS

KINDERGARDEN. Well not a lot happend here to be honest. I had a very joyfull time in kindergarden and spent alot of time outside. This habit of spending as much time outside the house as I can still stuck with me till today.

ELEMENTARY SCHOOL. In elementary school I've learned alot of valuable things. Not only about basic math and skills like writing and reading. Most importantly I learned strengths and weaknesses of myself. I noticed that I was much more talented in subjects that had to do with numbers rather than with words. I struggeled to indentify wheter a word was a subject or a verb in my german class but had no problems solving math problems that were meant for older students. A few friends I met here will also become and stay my best friends till today.

HIGH SCHOOL. The trend of liking numbers better than words continued in high school which in my country, Germany is called "Gymnasium". I choose a more scientific school that focused on teaching subjects like math, bioligy, chemistry, physics and computer science more than others. The last two of those subjects are also the subjects I succeeded in most during my school time. Except for those two tho I quickly realised that school was boring and our german school system broken. Good grades were not given to the best students but to the most likable and the test often did not require any understanding of the topics but were more of repeating facts you learned by heart the days before. Fortunatly I was gifted with the handy ability to remember everything I see and understsand quite quickly so exams were never a challange for me. The only subject I really liked in school was physics. Our teacher was great and because the interest in physics was low in my year we were tutored in a class of only 9 students which was amazing. In 2023 i graduated from my school with the highest degree my country offers: "Abitur".

TRAVEL

Alot of my friends decided to visit spain for a week or even two to get black out drunk and party everyday to celebrate their degree. Althoug there is nothing wrong with the idea of partying my stomach told me that this was a great opportunity to start an adventure that I would remember my entire life instead of getting drunk (and with my stomach I mean mostly my mom). So me and a friend I am proud to call one of my best friends to this day started to plan a trip to norway. We wandet to drive there by car and book playces to stay for the night on the fly when ever we felt like it. Well ofcourse not everything went as planned but still those weeks in the wild created some of the greatest memories of my life which i will never forget. I am forever grateful to my parents and grandparents who made this trip possible financially and Nils who i shared this experience with.
